Quick Omelet

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: cheese, chopped vegetables

Tip: Use fresh eggs for a fluffier omelet.

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a pan.
  3. Pour eggs, cook until set, add toppings.
  4. Fold omelet and serve hot.

Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add banana, milk, honey, and ice to blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy chilled.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper

Don’t skip toasting the bread for avocado toast.

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread on toast and serve.

Classic Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce and tomato
  • Turkey or chicken slices

Instructions

  1. Layer lettuce, tomato, and meat between bread slices.
  2. Add cheese.
  3. Cut diagonally and serve.

Chicken Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • Grilled chicken strips
  • Lettuce, tomato, cucumber
  • Yogurt, mayo, or buffalo dressing

Instructions

  1. Place chicken and veggies in tortilla.
  2. Drizzle with dressing.
  3. Wrap tightly and serve.

Quick Pasta Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (cooked)
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Optional: vegetables or meat

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta and drain.
  2. Mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Top with cheese and serve warm.

Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup cooked rice or noodles

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan, sauté garlic.
  2. Add vegetables, cook 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and serve with rice.

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with oil and spices.
  2. Grill for 5–7 minutes per side at 200°C.
  3. Serve with salad or rice.

One-Pot Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 cups broth
  • 1 scallion

Instructions

  1. Add rice, vegetables, chicken, and broth to pot.
  2. Cook until rice and chicken are done.
  3. Sprinkle with scallion.
  4. Serve hot.

Energy Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup of mixed nuts

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, peanut butter, honey, and mixed nuts.
  2. Press mixture into pan, refrigerate.
  3. Cut into bars and serve.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 apple & 1 banana
  • ½ cup grapes
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Chop all fruits into small pieces.
  2. Mix with lemon juice.
  3. Serve fresh.

Yogurt Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • ½ cup granola
  • Fresh fruit (berries, banana, etc.)
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add yogurt to bowl.
  2. Top with granola and fruit.
  3. Drizzle honey and serve.
